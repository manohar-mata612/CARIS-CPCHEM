=======================================================
INCIDENT: INC-2019-0342
=======================================================
Date: March 14, 2019
Equipment: CB-CGC-001 (Charge Gas Compressor 1)
Failure Code: MECH-BRG-IR (Inner Race Fault)
Downtime: 52 hours
Production Loss: $9.36M (52hrs x $180K/hr)

Pre-failure vibration readings (48 hours before failure):
  RMS:          4.8 mm/s  (threshold: 2.5)
  Peak:         16.2 mm/s (threshold: 8.0)
  Kurtosis:     11.3      (threshold: 3.5)
  Crest Factor: 6.8       (threshold: 4.0)

Root Cause: Contaminated lubrication oil. Water ingress
through deteriorated shaft seal allowed moisture into the
bearing housing, accelerating fatigue on inner race.

Corrective Actions:
  1. Replaced SKF 6314/C3 bearings (drive and non-drive end)
  2. Replaced shaft seal assembly
  3. Flushed and replaced lubrication oil (Mobil DTE Heavy)
  4. Installed oil moisture sensor
  5. Increased vibration monitoring frequency to daily

Lesson Learned: Kurtosis exceeded 8.0 threshold 72 hours
before failure. Earlier intervention at P2 level would have
prevented unplanned shutdown and reduced loss to ~$500K
(planned 4-hour bearing replacement during low production).

INCIDENT: INC-2021-0187
=======================================================
Date: July 8, 2021
Equipment: CB-QWP-001 (Quench Water Pump 1)
Failure Code: MECH-BRG-OR (Outer Race Fault)
Downtime: 18 hours
Production Loss: $3.24M

Pre-failure sensor readings (36 hours before failure):
  RMS:          5.2 mm/s
  Peak:         19.8 mm/s
  Kurtosis:     13.7
  Crest Factor: 7.6
  Bearing Temp: 108°C (normal: 70-85°C)

Root Cause: Static overloading during pump restart after
planned turnaround. Bearing installed with incorrect preload.

Corrective Actions:
  1. Replaced SKF NU314 ECP cylindrical roller bearings
  2. Updated installation procedure for preload specification
  3. Added mandatory vibration baseline check post-installation

INCIDENT: INC-2022-0445
=======================================================
Date: November 22, 2022
Equipment: CB-CGC-002 (Charge Gas Compressor 2)
Failure Code: MECH-BRG-RE (Rolling Element Fault)
Downtime: 31 hours
Production Loss: $5.58M

Pre-failure sensor readings (60 hours before failure):
  RMS:          3.9 mm/s
  Peak:         13.1 mm/s
  Kurtosis:     8.4
  Crest Factor: 5.6

Root Cause: Inadequate lubrication. Oil change interval
exceeded by 800 hours due to scheduling conflict during
turnaround. Oil viscosity degraded causing metal-to-metal
contact on rolling elements.

Corrective Actions:
  1. Replaced all bearings on CGC-002
  2. Implemented automated oil change scheduling in SAP PM
  3. Added oil viscosity sensor to continuous monitoring

INCIDENT: INC-2023-0091
=======================================================
Date: February 3, 2023
Equipment: CB-FDF-001 (Feed Furnace Fan 1)
Failure Code: MECH-BRG-IR
Downtime: 9 hours
Production Loss: $1.62M

Pre-failure sensor readings (84 hours before failure):
  RMS:          3.1 mm/s
  Kurtosis:     6.2
  Crest Factor: 5.1

Note: This incident was PARTIALLY PREVENTED. Vibration
monitoring caught the fault at P3 level (84hrs early).
Planned replacement was scheduled but could not be executed
before P1 threshold was reached due to parts availability.

Lesson Learned: Parts pre-positioning (SKF 6314/C3 bearings)
must be maintained on-site for all critical rotating equipment.
If parts had been available, downtime would have been 4 hours
instead of 9 hours, saving $900K.
