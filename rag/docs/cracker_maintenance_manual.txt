Operator: Chevron Phillips Chemical Company (CPChem)

=======================================================
SECTION 1: EQUIPMENT OVERVIEW
=======================================================
The Charge Gas Compressor (CGC) is the most critical rotating
equipment in the ethylene cracker. It compresses cracked gas
from the cracking furnaces to high pressure for downstream
separation. Any unplanned CGC shutdown stops ethylene production
entirely, causing losses of $180,000 per hour.

The CGC is a multi-stage centrifugal compressor driven by a
steam turbine. It operates at 8,500 RPM at full load with
inlet suction pressure of 0.8 bar and discharge pressure of
38 bar. Bearing temperature normal operating range is 70-90°C.
Vibration normal operating range is 0.5-2.5 mm/s RMS.

=======================================================
SECTION 2: BEARING FAULT DIAGNOSIS
=======================================================

2.1 INNER RACE FAULT (Failure Code: MECH-BRG-IR)
---------------------------------------------------
Symptoms:
  - Vibration RMS rises above 4.0 mm/s
  - Kurtosis value exceeds 8.0 (normal baseline: 2.5-3.5)
  - High-frequency impulses at bearing pass frequency (BPFI)
  - Peak vibration exceeds 12.0 mm/s
  - Crest factor above 6.0

Cause: Spalling or pitting on inner bearing race due to
contaminated lubrication, overloading, or fatigue.

Detection timeline: Inner race faults are detectable via
vibration analysis 48-96 hours before catastrophic failure.

Recommended action:
  P1 (kurtosis > 15, RMS > 6.0): Immediate controlled shutdown.
     Schedule emergency bearing replacement within 4 hours.
  P2 (kurtosis 8-15, RMS 4-6):   Plan bearing inspection within
     24 hours. Order replacement bearings SKF 6314/C3.
  P3 (kurtosis 5-8, RMS 2.5-4):  Increase monitoring frequency
     to every 4 hours. Schedule inspection at next opportunity.

Parts required: SKF 6314/C3 deep groove ball bearing x2
Estimated labor: 8 hours (2 technicians)
Safety: Isolate steam turbine, lock out tag out per SOP-CGC-004

2.2 OUTER RACE FAULT (Failure Code: MECH-BRG-OR)
---------------------------------------------------
Symptoms:
  - Vibration RMS rises above 3.5 mm/s
  - Kurtosis value exceeds 7.0
  - Bearing pass frequency outer race (BPFO) visible in spectrum
  - Crest factor above 5.5
  - Increased bearing temperature: 95-110°C

Cause: Contamination ingress, water in oil, electrical erosion,
or static overloading during installation.

Detection timeline: Outer race faults progress faster than inner
race faults. Detectable 24-72 hours before failure.

Recommended action:
  P1 (kurtosis > 12, RMS > 5.5): Immediate shutdown.
  P2 (kurtosis 7-12, RMS 3.5-5.5): Schedule within 12 hours.
  P3 (kurtosis 4-7, RMS 2.5-3.5): Monitor closely every 2 hours.

Parts required: SKF NU314 ECP cylindrical roller bearing x2
Estimated labor: 10 hours (2 technicians)

2.3 ROLLING ELEMENT (BALL) FAULT (Failure Code: MECH-BRG-RE)
--------------------------------------------------------------
Symptoms:
  - Vibration RMS rises above 3.0 mm/s
  - Kurtosis value exceeds 6.0
  - Ball pass frequency (BSF) sidebands visible
  - Crest factor above 5.0

Cause: Ball surface fatigue, inadequate lubrication,
manufacturing defects, or contamination.

Detection timeline: 36-84 hours before failure.

Recommended action:
  P1 (kurtosis > 10, RMS > 5.0): Immediate shutdown.
  P2 (kurtosis 6-10, RMS 3.0-5.0): Schedule within 24 hours.
  P3 (kurtosis 4-6, RMS 2.0-3.0): Monitor every 6 hours.

Parts required: SKF 6314/C3 x2, lubrication kit
Estimated labor: 6 hours

=======================================================
SECTION 3: LUBRICATION REQUIREMENTS
=======================================================
Lubricant: Mobil DTE Oil Heavy (ISO VG 100)
Change interval: Every 4,000 operating hours or 6 months
Oil temperature normal range: 45-65°C
Oil pressure normal range: 1.5-2.5 bar
Low oil pressure alarm: < 1.2 bar (P2 alert)
High oil temperature alarm: > 75°C (P2 alert)

=======================================================
SECTION 4: VIBRATION MONITORING THRESHOLDS
=======================================================
Parameter        | Normal      | Warning     | Critical
-----------------|-------------|-------------|----------
RMS (mm/s)       | 0.5 - 2.5  | 2.5 - 5.0  | > 5.0
Peak (mm/s)      | 1.5 - 8.0  | 8.0 - 15.0 | > 15.0
Kurtosis         | 2.5 - 3.5  | 3.5 - 8.0  | > 8.0
Crest Factor     | 2.5 - 4.0  | 4.0 - 6.0  | > 6.0
Bearing Temp (C) | 70 - 90    | 90 - 110   | > 110

=======================================================
SECTION 5: EMERGENCY SHUTDOWN PROCEDURE
=======================================================
1. Alert control room operator immediately
2. Reduce feed to cracking furnaces to minimum
3. Initiate controlled CGC speed reduction
4. Activate spare CGC-002 if available
5. Isolate steam supply to turbine
6. Apply LOTO per SOP-CGC-004
7. Notify maintenance supervisor and reliability engineer
8. Document all readings at time of shutdown
